	\section*{6.}
	$\Phi(N)=(p-1)\times(q-1),N=p\times q$，所以$\Phi(N)=p\times q-p-q+1=N-(p+q)+1$。所以可得到方程组:
	$$p\times q = N$$
	$$p+q = p\times q-\Phi(N)+1$$
	整理得$p^2-(N-\Phi(N)+1)p+N=0$
